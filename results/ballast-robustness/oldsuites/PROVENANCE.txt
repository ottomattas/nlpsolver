Old ballast suites — gpt+claude chunking provenance
====================================================

These are the 100-subset ballast suites at git rev 88ca7b0 (the
PRE-splitter-fix generation). They are NOT the suites at HEAD:
llmpipe/tests/ballast/tests_core_100_b{8,16}.py at HEAD are the
REGENERATED (post-fix) suites and differ case-by-case.

Why both exist
--------------
The collected gpt+claude b8/b16 baselines were run on the 88ca7b0
generation. To keep the chunking comparison (-slightcoarse and
-s2split -slightcoarse) case-identical for gpt+claude, their chunking
cells were run on THESE same suites, not the regenerated ones. The
gemini+deepseek baselines (Phase 3) and their chunking cells were run on
the regenerated HEAD suites, so for those models HEAD is the right pairing.

Validation does not require these files: the committed per-case JSONs
under core_100_b*_*/twostage/<llm>/ embed input_text, so dose_response.py
reconstructs accuracy self-contained. To confirm the case-identity claim
(each model's chunking cell used the SAME inputs as its baseline cell),
run the bundled check -- it pairs each model with its baseline and reports
same_input n/n plus the resolved suite rev (gpt+claude -> 88ca7b0,
gemini+deepseek -> HEAD):

  python3 analysis/dose_response.py -doses 8,16 \
      -tag s2split_slightcoarse -models gpt,claude,gemini,deepseek -provenance

These files are here only so the gpt+claude chunking cells can be RE-RUN
from HEAD without git archaeology:

  python3 runtests.py results/ballast-robustness/oldsuites/tests_core_100_b8.py \
      -llms gpt,claude -maxtokens 32000 -timeout 300 -slightcoarse -tag slightcoarse
  (and the -s2split -slightcoarse variant; b16 analogously)
